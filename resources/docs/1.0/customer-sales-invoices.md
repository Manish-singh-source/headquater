# Customer Sales Invoice Level

The **Customer Sales Invoice Level** report is used to check customer sales history invoice-wise.

This page shows sales order invoices with customer details, PO details, appointment details, dispatched quantity, tax values, payment status, paid amount, balance amount, and delivery document status.

---

## Purpose of Customer Sales Invoice Level

This report helps the admin review customer sales records at invoice level.

From this report, users can:

- View customer sales invoices.
- Check customer group, customer name, city, state, phone, and email.
- Review invoice number and PO number.
- Check appointment date and due date.
- Check POD, GRN, DN receipt, and LR status.
- Review ordered quantity, dispatched quantity, box count, and weight.
- Check taxable value, GST value, total invoice amount, paid amount, and balance amount.
- Filter records by date, customer, customer group, invoice number, PO number, and appointment date.
- Generate and download the filtered Excel report.

---

## Open Customer Sales Invoice Level

To open this report:

1. Open the main menu.
2. Go to **Reports**.
3. Open **Customer Sales**.
4. Click **Invoice Level**.

The system will open the `/customer-sales-invoices` page and show customer sales invoice records.

---

## Summary Cards

At the top of the page, the system shows invoice-level summary cards.

The summary includes:

- **Total Invoices**
- **Total Taxable Amount**
- **Total Amount**
- **Total Amount Paid**
- **Total Balance Amount**
- **Total Customers**

These values help the admin quickly review customer sales value, payment received, and pending balance.

---

## Filter Customer Sales Invoices

Use the filter section to search specific invoice records.

Available filters are:

1. **From Date**: Shows sales orders from this date.
2. **To Date**: Shows sales orders up to this date.
3. **Customer Name**: Shows records for selected customers.
4. **Customer Group**: Shows records for selected customer groups.
5. **Invoice No**: Shows selected invoice records.
6. **PO No**: Shows selected PO records.
7. **Appointment Date**: Shows invoices with selected appointment dates.

To apply filters:

1. Select the required filter values.
2. Click **Apply Filter**.
3. The table and summary values will update based on the selected filters.

To remove filters, click **Reset Filters**.

---

## Generate Report

To download the invoice-level report:

1. Apply filters if required.
2. Click **Generate Report**.
3. The system downloads an Excel report.

If filters are selected, the downloaded report contains only the filtered records. If no filter is selected, the report contains all available customer sales invoice-level records.

---

## When To Use This Report

Use **Customer Sales Invoice Level** when you need to check customer sales invoice details.

This report is useful for:

- Invoice-wise sales review
- Customer payment tracking
- Balance amount checking
- POD, GRN, DN, and LR verification
- Customer sales reconciliation
- Invoice report download

## Summary

The Customer Sales Invoice Level process follows these steps:

1. Open **Reports** from the menu.
2. Click **Customer Sales**.
3. Open **Invoice Level**.
4. Review summary cards.
5. Apply date, customer, customer group, invoice, PO, or appointment filters if required.
