# Vendor Purchase Invoice Level

The **Vendor Purchase Invoice Level** report is used to check vendor purchase history at invoice or purchase order level.

This page gives a summary of completed vendor purchase orders, invoice amounts, paid amounts, due amounts, GST values, invoice upload status, GRN upload status, and warehouse details.

---

## Purpose of Vendor Purchase Invoice Level

This report helps the admin review vendor purchase records after the vendor PI process is completed.

From this report, users can:

- View completed vendor purchase order records.
- Check vendor name and purchase order date.
- Review invoice reference, invoice date, and due date.
- Compare PO quantity and PI quantity.
- Check taxable value, GST amount, total amount, invoice amount, paid amount, and due amount.
- Check whether invoice and GRN files are uploaded.
- Filter records by date, purchase order number, and vendor.
- Generate and download the filtered report.

---

## Open Vendor Purchase Invoice Level

To open this report:

1. Open the main menu.
2. Go to **Reports**.
3. Open **Vendor Purchase History**.
4. Click **Invoice Level**.

The system will open the `/vendor-purchase-invoices` page and show vendor purchase invoice records.

---

## Summary Cards

At the top of the page, the system shows summary cards for the current report data.

The summary includes:

- **Total Vendor Orders**
- **Total Taxable Amount**
- **Total Amount**
- **Total Invoice Amount**
- **Total Paid Invoice Amount**
- **Total Due Invoice Amount**

These values help the admin quickly check the purchase value, invoice value, paid value, and pending due value.

---

## Filter Vendor Purchase Invoices

Use the filter section to search specific purchase records.

Available filters are:

1. **From Date**: Shows purchase orders created from this date.
2. **To Date**: Shows purchase orders created up to this date.
3. **Purchase Order No**: Shows selected purchase order records.
4. **Vendor Name**: Shows records for selected vendors.

To apply filters:

1. Select the required filter values.
2. Click **Apply Filter**.
3. The table and summary values will update based on the selected filters.

To remove filters, click **Reset Filters**.

---

## Vendor Purchase Invoice Records

The report table shows invoice-level purchase details.

Important columns include:

- Purchase order number
- Purchase order date
- Vendor name
- Invoice reference
- Invoice date
- Due date
- PO quantity
- PI quantity
- Taxable value
- GST, CGST, SGST, and IGST
- GST amount
- Total amount
- Invoice amount
- Invoice paid
- Invoice due
- Payment status
- Payment method
- Invoice uploaded status
- GRN uploaded status
- Shipping charges
- Warehouse

Use this table when invoice-wise purchase and payment tracking is required.

---

## Generate Report

To download the invoice-level report:

1. Apply filters if required.
2. Click **Generate Report**.
3. The system downloads a CSV report.

If filters are selected, the downloaded report contains only the filtered records. If no filter is selected, the report contains all available vendor purchase invoice-level records.

---

## When To Use This Report

Use **Vendor Purchase Invoice Level** when you need to check purchase data order-wise or invoice-wise.

This report is useful for:

- Vendor payment review
- Invoice due checking
- Purchase invoice reconciliation
- GRN and invoice upload verification
- Total vendor purchase value review

---

## Summary

The Vendor Purchase Invoice Level process follows these steps:

1. Open **Reports** from the menu.
2. Click **Vendor Purchase History**.
3. Open **Invoice Level**.
4. Review summary cards.
5. Apply date, purchase order, or vendor filters if required.
6. Check invoice-level purchase records.
7. Click **Generate Report** to download the CSV file.
