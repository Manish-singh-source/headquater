# Customers

The **Customers** page is used to create, view, and manage customer records. Customer details are used in sales orders, invoices, shipping addresses, and billing information.

---

## Open Customers

![Customers List](/docsimages/c1.png)

To open the Customers page:

1. Open the main menu.
2. Go to **Master**.
3. Click **Customers**.
4. The system will show the **Customers** page.

The Customers page is available from the `/customers` URL.

---

## Customers List

The customer list shows all customer records with contact, tax, and status details.

The table includes:

1. **Facility Name**
2. **Client Name**
3. **Contact Name**
4. **Email**
5. **Contact Number**
6. **GSTIN**
7. **PAN**
8. **Status**
9. **Action**

---

## Add Customer

![Customers List](/docsimages/c2.png)

To add a customer:

1. Click **Add Customer**.
2. Enter the customer details:
   - Facility name
   - Client name
   - Contact name
   - Email
   - Contact number
   - Company name
   - GST number
   - PAN number
3. Enter the **Shipping Address** details.
4. Enter the **Billing Address** details.
5. Select the customer **Status**.
6. Save the customer.

---

## Manage Customer

![Customers List](/docsimages/c3.png)

Use the action buttons to manage customer records:

1. Click **View** to open customer details.
2. Click **Edit** to update customer information.
3. Click **Delete** to remove a customer.
4. Use the status option to activate or deactivate a customer.
5. Use the bulk action menu to update or delete selected customers.

---

## Summary

The Customers process follows these steps:

1. Open **Master > Customers**.
2. Review the customer list.
3. Click **Add Customer** to create a customer.
4. Fill customer, shipping, and billing details.
5. View, edit, delete, or update status from the action buttons.
