# Products

The **Products** page is used to create, view, and manage product records and warehouse stock details. Product data is used in sales orders, purchase orders, invoices, stock management, and reports.

---

## Open Products

![Products List](/docsimages/pr1.png)

To open the Products page:

1. Open the main menu.
2. Go to **Master**.
3. Click **Products**.
4. The system will show the **Products** page.

The Products page is available from the `/products` URL.

---

## Products List

The products list shows product, vendor, warehouse, and inventory details.

The table includes:

1. **Warehouse**
2. **SKU Code**
3. **EAN Code**
4. **Brand**
5. **Brand Title**
6. **Category**
7. **PCS/Set**
8. **Sets/CTN**
9. **Weight**
10. **Case Pack Quantity**
11. **Vendor Code**
12. **Vendor Name**
13. **Vendor Purchase Rate**
14. **GST**
15. **HSN**
16. **Vendor Net Landing**
17. **Original Quantity**
18. **Available Quantity**
19. **Blocked Qty**
20. **Allocated Qty**
21. **PO Required**
22. **Date**
23. **Action**

---

## Add Products


![Products List](/docsimages/pr2.png)

To add products:

1. Click **Add Product**.
2. Select the **Warehouse**.
3. Upload the **Products Sheet** file.
4. Submit the form.

After submitting, the system will import products from the uploaded file and connect the stock with the selected warehouse.

---

## Manage Products


![Products List](/docsimages/pr3.png)

Use the action buttons to manage products:

1. Click **Edit** to update product information.
2. Click **Delete** to remove a product.
3. Use the bulk action menu to delete selected products.
4. Review **Available Quantity**, **Blocked Qty**, and **Allocated Qty** to understand stock position.
5. Check **PO Required** to know whether purchase order quantity is required.

---

## Summary

The Products process follows these steps:

1. Open **Master > Products**.
2. Review the product and stock list.
3. Click **Add Product** to upload a product sheet.
4. Select the warehouse and submit the file.
5. Edit, delete, or review stock details from the table.
