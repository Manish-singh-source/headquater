# Customer Groups

The **Customer Groups** page is used to create and manage customer groups. A customer group keeps related customers together and helps manage customer records, orders, and reports more easily.

---

## Open Customer Groups

![Customer Groups List](/docsimages/cg1.png)

To open the Customer Groups page:

1. Open the main menu.
2. Go to **Master**.
3. Click **Customer Groups**.
4. The system will show the **Customer Groups List** page.

The Customer Groups page is available from the `/customer-groups` URL.

---

## Customer Groups List

The list shows all customer groups with customer count and status information.

The table includes:

1. **Sr.No**
2. **Group Name**
3. **Total Customers**
4. **Active**
5. **Inactive**
6. **Created Date**
7. **Status**
8. **Actions**

Use this page to quickly check how many customers are connected with each group.

---

## Create Customer Group

![Customer Groups List](/docsimages/cg2.png)

To create a customer group:

1. Click **Create Group**.
2. Enter the **Customer Group Name**.
3. Upload the **Customers Data** file in Excel or CSV format.
4. Click **Submit**.

After submitting, the system will create the customer group and import the customer data from the uploaded file.

---

## Manage Customer Group

![Customer Groups List](/docsimages/cg3.png)

Use the action buttons to manage customer groups:

1. Click **View** to open group details.
2. Click **Edit** to update the group name or details.
3. Click **Delete** to remove the customer group.
4. Use the **Status** option to activate or deactivate the group.
5. Use the bulk action menu to update or delete selected groups.

---

## Summary

The Customer Groups process follows these steps:

1. Open **Master > Customer Groups**.
2. Review the group list.
3. Click **Create Group** to add a new group.
4. Upload customer data if required.
5. View, edit, delete, or update status from the action buttons.
