# Overview

**Headquaters** is an operations management software used to manage customers, vendors, products, purchase orders, sales orders, warehouse stock, invoices, dispatch, returns, and reports from one system.

It helps the team track the complete workflow from master data setup to order processing, stock movement, invoice generation, delivery updates, and payment tracking.

---

## What You Can Do

In this software, users can:

1. Manage master data such as customer groups, customers, vendors, products, SKU mapping, and warehouses.
2. Create and track sales orders.
3. Create and manage purchase orders.
4. Receive vendor products and update received product details.
5. Move products through packaging, ready-to-ship, and shipped stages.
6. Generate and download invoices.
7. Create manual invoices when required.
8. Review E-Invoice and E-Way Bill flow from invoice details.
9. Track vendor returns, customer returns, and product issues.
10. Track a sales order using the Track Order page.
11. Download Excel formats for bulk uploads.
12. View reports for purchase, inventory stock, and customer sales.

---

## Main Modules

The main modules available in the system are:

1. **Dashboard** for analytics and business summary.
2. **Access Control** for staff, roles, and permissions.
3. **Master** for customer groups, customers, vendors, products, SKU mapping, and warehouses.
4. **Purchase Workflow** for purchase order related process flow.
5. **Sales Order** for customer order creation and management.
6. **Track Order** for checking sales order status.
7. **Purchase Order** for vendor purchase order management.
8. **Received Products** for received stock updates.
9. **Packaging List** for packaging product management.
10. **Ready to Ship** for shipment preparation.
11. **Product Issues** for vendor and warehouse issue tracking.
12. **Vendor Return** for accepting or returning vendor products.
13. **Customer Return** for customer product returns.
14. **Invoice** for sales invoice generation and download.
15. **Manual Invoice** for creating invoices manually.
16. **E-Invoice** for reviewing E-Invoice and E-Way Bill flow.
17. **Excel Formats** for downloading upload formats.

---

## Basic Workflow

The common workflow follows these steps:

1. Add master data.
2. Create a sales order or purchase order.
3. Receive and manage product stock.
4. Package products and move them to ready-to-ship.
5. Generate invoices.
6. Update delivery, GRN, DN, and payment details.
7. Review reports and track order status.
